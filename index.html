Your body wasn't built to sit all day.

Two minutes an hour fixes more than you think. Work For Health.

What one hour of sitting does

  • 🩸 Circulation drops by half, blood pools in your lower legs
  • 🔥 Your metabolism slides into energy-saving mode
  • 🧈 The enzyme that burns fat in your bloodstream switches off
  • 🍬 Your muscles stop clearing sugar from your blood
  • 🦴 Pressure on your spinal discs climbs higher than standing

Heart

  • 54% higher heart attack risk
  • Stiffened arteries
  • Plaque buildup

Metabolism

  • Chronic high blood pressure
  • Midsection weight gain
  • Type 2 diabetes

Muscles and bones

  • Gluteal muscle wasting
  • Bone mineral loss
  • Early disc degeneration

Veins

  • Varicose veins
  • Deep vein blood clots

Why your gym session is not enough

Breaking up sitting every 30 to 60 minutes with even two minutes of light movement improves blood glucose and triglycerides, even when total daily activity is identical. Frequency beats intensity here. Small movements spread across your day are not a consolation prize. They are the thing itself.
